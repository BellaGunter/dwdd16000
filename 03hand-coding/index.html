Our Mission

Assist users in identifying their running goals and desires as well as educating beginners on the basics of form and practice of safety so they can accomplish their running goals!

The running world can be confusing at times, we cover the basics of different types of running and how to train for specific events, we explain basic injury prevention practices and techniques to work on and assist in making effective goals and action plans to accomplish them.

A Brief History On Running:

Running wasn't always a sport, especially long-distance running. People used to think the human body could only handle running about ten miles before it broke down but that all changed around 490 BC when Pheidippides in Athens ran a marathon to deliver an urgent message and then died of exhaustion.

This story was not forgotten and sparked the idea of long-distance racing which was first implemented in the ancient Olympic games around 776 BC. Today we break old norms of what we once thought the body was limited to and accept absurd challenges to the status quo through new forms of running and breaking record times. This is the heart of running, pursuing what is hard for the accomplishment of inner growth. Aren't you excited to get started?
